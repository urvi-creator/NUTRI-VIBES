Rice with Peas and Broccoli

This rice dish with peas and broccoli is a simple yet delicious meal that's perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. It's made with fluffy white rice, sweet peas, and tender broccoli florets, all seasoned with garlic and soy sauce for extra flavor.

To make this rice with peas and broccoli, you'll need:

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the white rice according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add frozen peas and broccoli florets to the skillet and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in cooked rice and soy sauce, and toss until everything is well combined.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.
  8. Enjoy your delicious rice with peas and broccoli!
