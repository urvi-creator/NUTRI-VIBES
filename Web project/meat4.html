Beef and Corn Soup

This beef and corn soup recipe is hearty, comforting, and perfect for chilly days. Tender beef is simmered with sweet corn, vegetables, and flavorful seasonings for a delicious soup that the whole family will love.

To make beef and corn soup, you'll need:

  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into small cubes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) corn kernels, drained
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, diced carrots, and diced celery to the pot. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the cubed beef stew meat to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Season the mixture with dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Pour in the beef broth and bring the soup to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 hour, or until the beef is tender.
  6. Add the drained corn kernels to the pot and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  8. Remove from heat and ladle the soup into bowls.
  9. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley, if desired.
  10. Enjoy your delicious beef and corn soup!
