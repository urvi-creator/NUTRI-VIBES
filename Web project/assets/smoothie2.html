Watermelon Smoothie

This watermelon smoothie is a refreshing and hydrating drink that's perfect for hot summer days. Made with juicy watermelon and a hint of lime, it's light, fruity, and incredibly delicious.

To make this watermelon smoothie, you'll need:

  • 2 cups chopped seedless watermelon
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Fresh lime juice to taste
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped watermelon, yogurt, honey (if using), and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.
  2. Add a handful of ice cubes to the blender.
  3. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour the smoothie into glasses and garnish with a slice of lime or a sprig of mint, if desired.
  5. Enjoy your refreshing watermelon smoothie!
