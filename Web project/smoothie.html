Strawberry Banana Smoothie

This strawberry banana smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious treat that's perfect for breakfast or a snack. Made with just three simple ingredients, it's quick and easy to whip up whenever you're craving something sweet and satisfying.

To make this strawberry banana smoothie, you'll need:

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

Instructions:

  1. Place the frozen strawberries, sliced banana, and milk in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, adding more milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
  4. Enjoy your delicious strawberry banana smoothie!
