Lemon Garlic Chicken and Green Beans

This lemon garlic chicken and green beans recipe is a quick and flavorful meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. Tender chicken breasts are marinated in a zesty lemon garlic sauce and roasted alongside fresh green beans for a healthy and delicious dish.

To make lemon garlic chicken and green beans, you'll need:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.
  2. Place the chicken breasts and trimmed green beans in a large resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken and green beans, ensuring they are evenly coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours.
  3. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Remove the chicken and green beans from the marinade and transfer them to a baking dish or sheet pan. Discard any remaining marinade.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the green beans are tender.
  6. Remove from the oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley, if desired.
  8. Serve and enjoy your lemon garlic chicken and green beans!
