Tuscan Chicken and Green Beans

This Tuscan chicken and green beans recipe is a delicious and easy one-pan meal. Tender chicken breasts are cooked with fresh green beans, cherry tomatoes, and Italian seasoning for a flavorful dish that's perfect for busy weeknights.

To make Tuscan chicken and green beans, you'll need:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chicken breasts, green beans, cherry tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper until everything is evenly coated.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish or sheet pan, arranging the chicken breasts in a single layer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Remove from the oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley, if desired.
  7. Serve and enjoy your Tuscan chicken and green beans!
