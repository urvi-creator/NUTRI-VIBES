Potato Salad

This classic potato salad is a staple at summer barbecues and picnics. Made with tender potatoes, crunchy celery, and creamy dressing, it's sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

To make this potato salad, you'll need:

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, boil the diced potatoes until tender, about 10-15 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled potatoes, chopped eggs, diced celery, chopped red onion, and chopped parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, white vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the potato mixture and toss until well coated.
  5. Chill the potato salad in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Enjoy your delicious potato salad!
