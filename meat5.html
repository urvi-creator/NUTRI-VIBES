Grounded Turkey Recipe

This grounded turkey recipe is a flavorful and healthy alternative to traditional ground beef dishes. Made with lean ground turkey, aromatic vegetables, and savory spices, it's a versatile dish that can be used in various recipes.

To make this grounded turkey, you'll need:

  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper to the skillet. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add ground turkey to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks.
  4. Season the turkey mixture with dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Continue cooking until the turkey is fully cooked through and the flavors are well combined.
  6. Remove from heat and use the grounded turkey in your favorite recipes, such as tacos, pasta sauces, or stuffed peppers.
  7. Enjoy your delicious grounded turkey!
